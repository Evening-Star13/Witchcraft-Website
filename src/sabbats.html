Sabbats & Esbats

In the haunting embrace of the moon's glow, our Sabbats and Esbats awaken the ancient rhythms of the earth. Each gathering is a communion with the shadows, where the wheel of the year turns, and the veil between realms thins. Under the watchful eyes of forgotten deities, we dance in circles, invoking the spirits of nature and the echoes of the past. Beware, for in these sacred rites, the line between celebration and sacrifice blurs, and the whispers of the night may reveal truths best left undisturbed.
