Prayers & Invocations

In the hushed shadows of twilight, our prayers rise like smoke, entwining with the whispers of the damned. Each invocation is a thread woven into the fabric of the abyss, calling forth ancient entities that linger in the void. With trembling lips, the faithful beseech powers that dwell beyond the veil, seeking solace or vengeance. Beware, for the echoes of these sacred words may awaken slumbering horrors, and the price of communion could be your very soul.
